See the Difference with Our 4-Stage Filtration:

Stage 1 Filtration

A washable pre-filter that removes large particles such as dust mites, pet hair and dirt.

Stage 2 Filtration

A complex fibrous anti-virus filter that is proven to combat the growth of mold, bacteria and airborne viruses.

Stage 3 Filtration

An industry leading 3M HEPA filter that traps 99.99% of airborne particles down to 0.3 microns such as, pollen, bacteria, pet dander, dust particles and other pollutants.

Stage 4 Filtration

A porous activated carbon filter that absorbs and reduces tobacco smoke, household odors, gases, chemicals, VOC's and fumes.

PureAir 2-in-1 HEPA-Carbon

The PureAir HEPA-Carbon filter is a 2-in-1 HEPA and Activated Carbon filter. Our 3M HEPA filter traps 99.99% of harmful particles above 0.3 microns while standard HEPA filters trap just 99.97% of particles above 0.3 microns. The added element of activated carbon also gives this filter the ability to absorb household odors, smoke and chemical vapors. With the advanced PureAir 2-in-1 HEPA-Carbon filter, you'll only have to replace one filter instead of two saving you time and money.

PureAir Anti-Virus

The PureAir Anti-Virus filter contains antimicrobial agents that combat the growth of mold, bacteria and viruses. This filter provides immune system support as it removes harmful contagions from your air. The PureAir Anti-Virus filter is an excellent choice for ensuring cleaner, healthier air for your family's bedrooms or child's nursery.
